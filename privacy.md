# Privacy & Cookie Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-10_

This page explains what data joernberkefeld.com collects, which cookies are used,
and how you stay in control.

affiliated with or endorsed by any employer, client, or Salesforce. Contact:
[joern.berkefeld@gmail.com](mailto:joern.berkefeld@gmail.com).

## Hosting

The site is served as static files via GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). Like any web
host, GitHub processes your IP address and request metadata to deliver pages and
to protect the service. See the
[GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
for details.

## Cookies

The consent tool that shows the cookie banner is self-hosted on this domain — it
loads no third-party scripts and sets no tracking cookies of its own.

| Cookie | Category | Purpose | Retention |
|---|---|---|---|
| `cc_cookie` | Strictly necessary | Remembers your cookie choice so the banner is not shown again. | 182 days |
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Analytics (opt-in) | Google Analytics — distinguishes visitors. Set **only** if you accept analytics. | up to 2 years |
| `_gid` | Analytics (opt-in) | Google Analytics — distinguishes visitors. Set **only** if you accept analytics. | 24 hours |

Strictly necessary cookies are always active because the site cannot remember
your preference without them. All other cookies require your explicit consent.

## Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (Google Ireland Ltd.) to understand how it is
used. Analytics is **disabled by default** and is only activated after you click
**Accept** in the cookie banner. This is implemented with
[Google Consent Mode v2](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9976101):
until you consent, `analytics_storage` is set to `denied`, so no analytics
cookies are set and no analytics data is collected.

If you accept, Google Analytics collects aggregated, pseudonymised usage
statistics such as pages viewed, approximate location (derived from IP, which
Google truncates), device type, and referring site. No advertising or
personalisation signals are enabled. Data may be processed by Google in
accordance with the
[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Changing or withdrawing consent

You can change your choice at any time using the **Manage cookies** link in the
site footer. Declining — or withdrawing — consent stops further analytics
collection and clears the analytics cookies. You can also delete cookies through
your browser settings.

## Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and object
to the processing of your personal data, and to data portability. Because this
site only processes data through the third parties named above, exercise these
rights with the relevant provider (Google, GitHub) or contact me at the email
above and I will help where I can.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the site evolves. The "last updated" date at the
top reflects the most recent change.
